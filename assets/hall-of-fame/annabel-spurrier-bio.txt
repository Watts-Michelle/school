Annabel Spurrier qualified in all aspects of Film, TV and Theatre makeup and hair including wig fitting, wig dressing and styling. She has gone on to work for films and TV including feature films The Manual, and Seamonsters, and short films including Lifestyle Concept, The Old Boys, Allema, Hit or Miss, They Walk Among Us, and Meeting Monica.
She was lucky enough to work with the still very beautiful Rita Tushingham recently in a feature, and regularly does hair and make-up for the BAFTAs. She also recently worked with a client for an Elton John event.
